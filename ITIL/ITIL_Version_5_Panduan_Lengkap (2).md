## 1️⃣ RINGKASAN EKSEKUTIF

### Apa Itu ITIL Version 5?

ITIL Version 5 adalah evolusi dari ITIL 4 yang dirilis pada **Januari 2026**. Framework ini dirancang khusus untuk era digital, AI-driven, dan fokus pada manajemen produk & layanan digital secara terintegrasi.

**Target Audience:** 
- IT professionals
- Product managers
- Service managers
- Business leaders
- Organizational change managers

## 2️⃣ PERBEDAAN UTAMA ITIL V4 vs ITIL V5

### Tabel Perbandingan Komprehensif

| Aspek | ITIL V4 | ITIL V5 |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Total Practices** | 34 (17 service, 14 general, 3 technical) | 34 (22 product & service, 12 general) |
| **Service Mgmt Practices** | 17 | 22 ⬆️ |
| **General Mgmt Practices** | 14 | 12 ⬇️ |
| **Technical Mgmt Practices** | 3 (terpisah) | ❌ Dihilangkan (merged) |
| **Focus Utama** | IT Service Management | Digital Product & Service Mgmt |
| **AI Integration** | Minimal | ✅ AI-Native & AI Governance |
| **Scope** | Primarily IT-focused | Enterprise-wide (IT + Product + Business) |
| **Structure** | Process-centric | Lifecycle-centric & Outcome-focused |
| **Exam Format** | Mostly closed-book | Foundation: Closed-book; Others: Open-book |
| **Digital Experience** | Implicit | ✅ Core focus |
| **Cross-functional** | Limited focus | ✅ Integrated teams |

### Perubahan Struktural Utama

#### ✂️ Technical Management Practices DIHILANGKAN
Tiga practices yang dulunya di Technical Management pindah ke Product & Service Management:
1. **Deployment Management**
2. **Infrastructure and Platform Management**
3. **Software Development and Management**

#### 🔄 Practices yang Berubah Lokasi
- **Information Security Management** → dari General ke Product & Service
- **Service Financial Management** → dari General ke Product & Service

## 3️⃣ 34 MANAGEMENT PRACTICES ITIL VERSION 5

### **A. PRODUCT AND SERVICE MANAGEMENT PRACTICES (22 Practices)**

Practices untuk mengelola produk dan layanan digital secara end-to-end.

#### 1. **Availability Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan service memberikan tingkat ketersediaan (availability) sesuai kesepakatan untuk memenuhi kebutuhan pelanggan
- **Focus:** Mengoptimalkan uptime dan reliability
- **Key Metrics:** Uptime %, MTBF, MTTR

#### 2. **Business Analysis**
- **Definisi:** Menganalisis bisnis, mengidentifikasi kebutuhan, dan merekomendasikan solusi untuk mengatasi masalah bisnis
- **Focus:** Problem-solving & value creation
- **Key Roles:** Business analyst, stakeholder management

#### 3. **Capacity and Performance Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan service mencapai performance level yang disepakati sambil memuaskan demand saat ini dan masa depan dengan cost-effective
- **Focus:** Resource optimization, performance tuning
- **Key Activities:** Capacity planning, bottleneck analysis

#### 4. **Change Enablement**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan service mencapai performance levels yang diharapkan dengan memuaskan demand saat ini dan masa depan
- **Focus:** Managing changes in controlled manner
- **Key Aspects:** Risk assessment, communication, training

#### 5. **Deployment Management** ✨ (Pindahan dari Technical)
- **Definisi:** Memindahkan hardware, software, dokumentasi, proses, atau komponen service ke controlled environment
- **Focus:** Release deployment, rollback procedures
- **Key Phases:** Pre-deployment, deployment, post-deployment

#### 6. **Incident Management**
- **Definisi:** Meminimalkan dampak negatif incident dengan mengembalikan operasi service normal secepat mungkin
- **Focus:** Speed & quality of resolution
- **Key Metrics:** MTTx (Mean Time To X), incident volume

#### 7. **Information Security Management** ✨ (Dari General Management)
- **Definisi:** Policy yang mengatur pendekatan organisasi terhadap information security management
- **Focus:** Confidentiality, integrity, availability
- **Key Aspects:** Risk assessment, access control, compliance

#### 8. **Infrastructure and Platform Management** ✨ (Pindahan dari Technical)
- **Definisi:** Mengawasi infrastruktur dan platform yang digunakan organisasi
- **Focus:** Technology solutions, third-party integrations
- **Key Activities:** Infrastructure planning, monitoring, optimization

#### 9. **IT Asset Management**
- **Definisi:** Merencanakan dan mengelola lifecycle lengkap dari semua IT assets
- **Focus:** Asset tracking, lifecycle management
- **Key Metrics:** Asset inventory, ROI, TCO

#### 10. **Monitoring and Event Management**
- **Definisi:** Secara sistematis mengobservasi services dan service components, serta mencatat & melaporkan perubahan state yang teridentifikasi sebagai events
- **Focus:** Real-time monitoring, event correlation
- **Key Tools:** Monitoring tools, alerting systems

#### 11. **Problem Management**
- **Definisi:** Mengurangi likelihood dan impact incident dengan mengidentifikasi root causes dan mengelola workarounds
- **Focus:** Root cause analysis, permanent solutions
- **Key Activities:** Problem investigation, KB updates

#### 12. **Release Management**
- **Definisi:** Membuat service baru dan service yang telah berubah tersedia untuk digunakan
- **Focus:** Release planning, coordination
- **Key Phases:** Release planning, build, test, deployment

#### 13. **Service Catalog Management**
- **Definisi:** Menyediakan single source informasi konsisten tentang semua services dan service offerings
- **Focus:** Service information accuracy & accessibility
- **Key Deliverables:** Service catalog, service portfolio

#### 14. **Service Configuration Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan informasi akurat dan reliable tentang konfigurasi services dan configuration items tersedia ketika dibutuhkan
- **Focus:** Configuration accuracy, CMDB management
- **Key Aspects:** CI relationships, version control

#### 15. **Service Continuity Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan availability dan performance services terjaga di level sufficient dalam case disaster
- **Focus:** Disaster recovery, business continuity
- **Key Plans:** BCP, DRP, RTO, RPO

#### 16. **Service Design**
- **Definisi:** Mendesain products dan services yang fit for purpose, fit for use, dan dapat delivered oleh organisasi dan ecosystem-nya
- **Focus:** Design quality, fitness for use
- **Key Phases:** Requirements, design, validation

#### 17. **Service Desk**
- **Definisi:** Menyediakan entry point dan single point of contact dengan service provider untuk users, dan capturing demand untuk incident resolution & service requests
- **Focus:** User satisfaction, first contact resolution
- **Key Metrics:** Customer satisfaction, FCR

#### 18. **Service Financial Management** ✨ (Dari General Management)
- **Definisi:** Mendukung strategi organisasi dengan memastikan resources financial digunakan secara efektif
- **Focus:** Cost management, budget control
- **Key Activities:** Cost allocation, chargeback models

#### 19. **Service Level Management**
- **Definisi:** Menetapkan clear business-based targets untuk service levels dan memastikan service delivery dinilai, dimonitor, dan dikelola terhadap targets
- **Focus:** SLA creation, performance measurement
- **Key Aspects:** SLA negotiation, SLO monitoring

#### 20. **Service Request Management**
- **Definisi:** Menangani semua predefined, user-initiated service requests dengan cara yang efektif dan user-friendly
- **Focus:** Request fulfillment, self-service
- **Key Tools:** Service portal, ticketing system

#### 21. **Service Validation and Testing**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan service baru atau yang berubah memenuhi defined requirements
- **Focus:** Quality assurance, testing
- **Key Activities:** Test planning, test execution, sign-off

#### 22. **Software Development and Management** ✨ (Pindahan dari Technical)
- **Definisi:** Memastikan applications memenuhi stakeholder needs dalam hal functionality, reliability, maintainability, compliance, dan auditability
- **Focus:** Application lifecycle, code quality
- **Key Aspects:** Development processes, testing, deployment

### **B. GENERAL MANAGEMENT PRACTICES (12 Practices)**

Practices yang berlaku untuk semua aspek organisasi, tidak hanya IT/Service.

#### 1. **Architecture Management**
- **Definisi:** Memberikan understanding tentang berbagai elemen organisasi dan bagaimana mereka saling berhubungan
- **Focus:** Organizational design, system thinking

#### 2. **Continual Improvement**
- **Definisi:** Menyelaraskan practices dan services organisasi dengan changing business needs melalui ongoing identification dan improvement
- **Focus:** Kaizen mindset, improvement culture
- **Key Model:** 7-step improvement process

#### 3. **Knowledge Management**
- **Definisi:** Mempertahankan dan meningkatkan effective, efficient, dan convenient use of information dan knowledge di organisasi
- **Focus:** Knowledge capture, sharing, reuse
- **Key Tools:** Knowledge base, wiki, documentation

#### 4. **Measurement and Reporting**
- **Definisi:** Mendukung good decision-making dan continual improvement dengan menurunkan uncertainty levels
- **Focus:** Metrics, analytics, reporting
- **Key Activities:** Data collection, analysis, reporting

#### 5. **Organizational Change Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan perubahan di organisasi diimplementasikan smoothly dan successfully dengan mencapai lasting benefits
- **Focus:** People aspects, change adoption
- **Key Phases:** Awareness, understanding, acceptance, change

#### 6. **Portfolio Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan organisasi memiliki right mix dari programmes, projects, products, dan services untuk execute strategy
- **Focus:** Strategic alignment, resource allocation
- **Key Activities:** Portfolio planning, prioritization

#### 7. **Project Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan semua projects organisasi successfully delivered
- **Focus:** Project execution, delivery
- **Key Aspects:** Scope, schedule, budget, quality

#### 8. **Relationship Management**
- **Definisi:** Membangun dan memelihara links antara organisasi dan stakeholders di level strategic dan tactical
- **Focus:** Stakeholder engagement, partnerships
- **Key Activities:** Communication, stakeholder management

#### 9. **Risk Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan organisasi understands dan effectively handles risks
- **Focus:** Risk identification, assessment, mitigation
- **Key Phases:** Identify, analyze, respond, monitor

#### 10. **Strategy Management**
- **Definisi:** Merumuskan goals organisasi dan adopting courses of action dan resource allocation untuk mencapainya
- **Focus:** Strategic planning, vision & mission
- **Key Outcomes:** Strategic plan, roadmap

#### 11. **Supplier Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan suppliers dan performance levels mereka dikelola appropriately untuk support provision of seamless quality products dan services
- **Focus:** Vendor management, SLA enforcement
- **Key Aspects:** Vendor selection, performance review

#### 12. **Workforce and Talent Management**
- **Definisi:** Memastikan organisasi memiliki people yang tepat dengan appropriate skills dan knowledge di correct roles
- **Focus:** HR practices, talent development
- **Key Activities:** Recruitment, training, development

## 4️⃣ FITUR UTAMA & INOVASI ITIL V5

### 🤖 **AI-Native & AI Governance**
- Built-in guidance untuk adopting AI responsibly
- Dedicated ITIL AI Governance extension module
- Risk assessment dan opportunity identification
- Ethical AI frameworks

### 📱 **Digital Product & Service Focus**
- Unified lifecycle untuk digital products dan services
- End-to-end journey mapping
- Customer experience sebagai core focus
- Omnichannel delivery

### 🔄 **Lifecycle Mindset**
- Integrated approach dari strategy hingga operations
- Elimination of silos antar teams
- Value stream visibility
- Continuous feedback loops

### 👥 **Cross-functional Collaboration**
- Team-based delivery models
- Shared outcomes & objectives
- Transparent communication
- Value co-creation with stakeholders

### 📈 **Outcome-Oriented**
- Fokus pada value delivery bukan process execution
- Measurable business outcomes
- Better alignment dengan business goals
- Impact-driven improvements

### 🌐 **Enterprise-Wide Applicability**
- Tidak hanya IT departments
- Berlaku untuk product teams, business units
- Scalable ke berbagai industry
- Adaptable to different organizational structures

## 7️⃣ FAQ - PERTANYAAN UMUM

### Q: Apakah ITIL 4 certification saya masih valid?
**A:** Ya, ITIL 4 Foundation tetap valid sebagai prerequisite untuk ITIL V5 advanced modules. Anda tidak perlu retake Foundation jika sudah punya ITIL 4 Foundation. Untuk advanced levels, transition pathways tersedia.

### Q: Bagaimana cara transition dari ITIL 4 ke ITIL 5?
**A:** 
- **ITIL 4 Foundation holders:** Langsung ke advanced ITIL V5 modules
- **ITIL 4 Practice Manager:** Complete ITIL Transformation module
- **ITIL 4 Managing Professional:** Complete ITIL Managing Professional Transition (MPT)
- **ITIL 4 Strategic Leader:** Complete ITIL Transformation module
- **ITIL 4 Master:** Complete ITIL Managing Professional Transition

### Q: Apakah training mandatory untuk ITIL V5 Foundation?
**A:** Tidak, PeopleCert tidak mandate training untuk Foundation. Self-study diperbolehkan. Namun, accredited training sangat direkomendasi untuk pemahaman penuh.

### Q: Berapa lama belajar ITIL V5?
**A:** 
- Foundation: 3-5 hari training + self-study
- Advanced modules: 5-7 hari training
- Total untuk Practice Manager: 2-3 bulan
- Total untuk Master: 6-12 bulan (tergantung path)

### Q: Apakah ada requirement khusus untuk AI Governance module?
**A:** Tidak, AI Governance adalah extension module yang tidak require ITIL Foundation sebagai prerequisite, tapi highly recommended.

### Q: Berapa cost ITIL V5 certification?
**A:** 
- Foundation exam: ±$150-300 USD
- Advanced modules: ±$300-500 USD each
- Training courses: ±$500-2000 USD (tergantung provider & format)
- PeopleCert Plus membership: ±$200-400/tahun

### Q: Kapan ITIL 4 akan retire?
**A:** ITIL 4 akan berjalan parallel dengan ITIL V5 untuk periode transisi. Timeline retirement belum ditentukan secara pasti.

### Q: Apakah ITIL V5 cocok untuk non-IT organizations?
**A:** Ya, ITIL V5 dirancang untuk semua organisasi yang mengelola products & services, tidak hanya IT.

### Q: Bisakah saya skip modules dalam ITIL V5?
**A:** 
- Foundation: Mandatory untuk semua
- Advanced modules: No prescribed order setelah Foundation
- Transformation: Must complete untuk semua designations

### **Key Changes from V4 to V5**

✅ **Added/Enhanced:**
- AI governance & guidance
- Digital product management
- Experience management
- Lifecycle approach
- Cross-functional integration

⬇️ **Removed/Consolidated:**
- Technical Management Practices (merged to Product & Service)
- Some standalone practitioner certifications

📈 **Evolved:**
- More practical, less process-heavy
- Open-book exams (except Foundation)
- Outcome-focused vs. task-focused
