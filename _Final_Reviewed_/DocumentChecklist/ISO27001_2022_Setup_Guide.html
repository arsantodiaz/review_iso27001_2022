1. Pengenalan ISO 27001:2022

1.1 Apa itu ISO 27001:2022?

ISO 27001:2022 adalah standar internasional untuk Information Security Management System (ISMS). Standar ini menggantikan ISO 27001:2013 dan membawa perubahan signifikan dalam pendekatan keamanan informasi.

💡 Poin Penting: ISO 27001:2022 berfokus pada konteks organisasi, stakeholder management, dan risk-based approach yang lebih holistik.

1.2 Perubahan Utama dari Versi Sebelumnya

Aspek ISO 27001:2013 ISO 27001:2022
Jumlah Kontrol 114 kontrol 93 kontrol (disederhanakan)
Fokus Utama Compliance checklist Risk-based dan konteks organisasi
Cloud & Outsourcing Terbatas Lebih detail dan komprehensif
Supply Chain Minimal Fokus pada supply chain security
Ukuran Organisasi Enterprise-centric Fleksibel untuk semua ukuran

1.3 Manfaat Implementasi

  • Perlindungan Data: Melindungi informasi sensitif dari ancaman cyber
  • Kredibilitas Bisnis: Meningkatkan kepercayaan klien dan partner
  • Compliance: Memenuhi persyaratan regulasi (GDPR, PDP, dll)
  • Efisiensi Operasional: Proses keamanan yang terstruktur dan efisien
  • Keunggulan Kompetitif: Diferensiasi dalam tender dan partnership
  • Risk Reduction: Mengurangi potensi kerugian dari security incidents

2. Kerangka Kerja & Struktur ISO 27001:2022

2.1 Struktur Tingkat Tinggi (High Level Structure)

ISO 27001:2022 menggunakan High Level Structure (HLS) yang sama dengan standar manajemen ISO lainnya:

Annex SL Framework:
  1. Organisasi dan Konteks: Memahami organisasi Anda
  2. Kepemimpinan: Komitmen dan dukungan manajemen
  3. Perencanaan: Identifikasi risiko dan perencanaan strategis
  4. Dukungan: Sumber daya, kompetensi, komunikasi, dokumentasi
  5. Operasi: Implementasi dan pengendalian
  6. Evaluasi Kinerja: Monitoring, measurement, audit, management review
  7. Perbaikan: Peningkatan berkelanjutan

2.2 Organisasi Kontrol dalam ISO 27001:2022

3. Fase 1: Persiapan & Assessment (0-4 minggu)

3.1 Langkah-Langkah Awal

3.1.1 Mendapatkan Dukungan Manajemen (C-Level)

Langkah pertama dan paling kritis adalah mendapatkan executive sponsorship:

  • Presentasi kepada CEO/Board tentang business case
  • Jelaskan ROI dan risk mitigation
  • Butuh budget, resources, dan time commitment
  • Tetapkan steering committee dengan C-level member
⚠️ KRITIS: Tanpa dukungan manajemen, implementasi akan gagal. Pastikan ISO 27001 adalah prioritas strategis organisasi.

3.1.2 Bentuk Tim Project

Struktur organisasi yang direkomendasikan:

  • Steering Committee: CFO, COO, CIO, Head of HR
  • Information Security Officer (ISO): Lead implementasi
  • Process Owners: Mewakili setiap departemen
  • External Consultant: Opsional, membantu accelerate
  • Internal Audit Team: Verifikasi compliance

3.1.3 Conduct Current State Assessment

Lakukan audit menyeluruh terhadap status keamanan saat ini:

  • Identify existing security policies dan procedures
  • Assess infrastructure keamanan (hardware, software, network)
  • Review access control dan user management
  • Evaluate incident response capability
  • Check existing documentation dan records

Deliverable: Gap Analysis Report menunjukkan perbedaan antara current state dan ISO 27001:2022 requirements

3.2 Define Scope ISMS

📌 Scope Definition adalah kunci: Tentukan dengan jelas apa yang termasuk dan tidak termasuk dalam ISMS.

Contoh scope definition:

ISMS Scope untuk XYZ Bank: - Aplikasi Core Banking dan Internet Banking - Data center dan infrastructure di Jakarta - Semua pengguna internal (500 orang) - Partnership dengan payment gateway providers - DIKECUALIKAN: Remote office di daerah, legacy system yang deprecated

3.3 Tentukan ISMS Policy Framework

  • Information Security Policy (master policy)
  • Access Control Policy
  • Data Classification Policy
  • Incident Response Policy
  • Acceptable Use Policy
  • Change Management Policy

3.4 Timeline Fase 1

Minggu 1
Executive kickoff meeting, form governance structure
Minggu 2
Process mapping, stakeholder interviews, current state assessment
Minggu 3
Gap analysis, draft scope definition, policy framework planning
Minggu 4
Finalize gap analysis, present findings, get approval untuk Phase 2

4. Fase 2: Perencanaan & Desain (4-8 minggu)

4.1 Risk Assessment & Treatment

4.1.1 Identifikasi Assets

Langkah pertama dalam risk assessment:

  • Information assets (data, databases)
  • Technology assets (servers, networks, applications)
  • Physical assets (facilities, equipment)
  • People/personnel

4.1.2 Identifikasi Threats & Vulnerabilities

Threat Type Contoh Severity
Cyber Attacks Ransomware, malware, phishing CRITICAL
Insider Threats Unauthorized access, data theft HIGH
Physical Security Theft, unauthorized entry MEDIUM-HIGH
System Failure Hardware failure, outage MEDIUM
Compliance Breach Data exposure, violation HIGH

4.1.3 Risk Evaluation & Treatment Planning

Untuk setiap risk, tentukan treatment strategy:

  • Mitigate: Implement control untuk reduce risk
  • Accept: Terima risk (dengan approval)
  • Avoid: Stop activity yang cause risk
  • Transfer: Transfer via insurance atau outsourcing

Deliverable: Risk Register dengan semua identified risks, likelihood, impact, treatment plan

4.2 Control Selection & Design

Berdasarkan risk assessment, pilih controls yang diperlukan dari ISO 27001:2022 Annex A:

⚡ Best Practice: Jangan implement semua 93 kontrol. Lakukan risk-based selection. Organisasi yang berbeda akan memiliki kontrol yang berbeda berdasarkan risk profile mereka.

4.3 Develop ISMS Policies & Procedures

Develop atau update comprehensive policies yang cover:

  • Information Classification & Labeling
  • Access Control & User Management
  • Change Management
  • Incident Management
  • Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery
  • Supplier/Third-party Management
  • Audit & Monitoring

4.4 Design Technical & Organizational Controls

Technical Controls (Examples):

  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Encryption (data at rest & in transit)
  • Firewalls & Intrusion Detection Systems
  • Backup & disaster recovery systems
  • Security monitoring & logging

Organizational Controls (Examples):

  • Security awareness training program
  • Incident response team & procedures
  • Third-party security assessment
  • Periodic security reviews
  • Management review meetings

4.5 Develop Implementation Roadmap

Create detailed roadmap untuk Phase 3:

  • Quick wins (mudah, impact tinggi) - implement first
  • Medium-term initiatives (3-6 bulan)
  • Long-term strategic projects (6-12 bulan)
  • Budget allocation per initiative
  • Resource planning & assignment

5. Fase 3: Implementasi (8-24 minggu)

5.1 Establish ISMS Documentation System

Buat struktur dokumentasi yang terorganisir:

Documentation Structure: ├── Level 1: ISMS Charter & Scope ├── Level 2: Information Security Policy ├── Level 3: Procedures & Guidelines │ ├── Access Management Procedure │ ├── Change Management Procedure │ ├── Incident Response Procedure │ └── ... ├── Level 4: Work Instructions & Forms │ ├── User Access Request Form │ ├── System Change Request Form │ └── ... └── Level 5: Records & Evidence ├── Risk Assessment Records ├── Audit Results └── Training Records

5.2 Implement Technical Controls

5.2.1 Priority Technical Controls

  • Access Control: Implement role-based access control (RBAC), segregation of duties
  • Authentication: Deploy MFA across all systems
  • Encryption: Encrypt sensitive data at rest dan in transit
  • Monitoring: Implement SIEM atau centralized logging
  • Backup: Establish robust backup & recovery procedures

5.3 Implement Organizational Controls

  • Launch security awareness campaign
  • Conduct mandatory security training (quarterly minimum)
  • Establish incident response team & hotline
  • Implement change advisory board (CAB)
  • Perform regular security audits (internal)
  • Conduct risk assessment reviews (semi-annual)

5.4 Monitor Implementation Progress

Control Target Date Status Owner
MFA Implementation End of Month 2 On Track IT Director
Data Encryption End of Month 3 At Risk Security Ops
Incident Response Plan End of Month 2 Completed CISO

5.5 Change Management During Implementation

💡 Tip: Implementasi ISO 27001 akan membuat perubahan signifikan dalam cara kerja. Manage change dengan hati-hati melalui change management formal, communication, dan training.

5.6 Stakeholder Engagement & Communication

  • Monthly steering committee meetings
  • Bi-weekly project status updates
  • Quarterly town halls untuk staff awareness
  • Regular communication tentang security incidents (lessons learned)

6. Fase 4: Monitoring & Improvement (Ongoing)

6.1 Establish KPIs & Metrics

KPI Target Frequency
Security Incident Response Time <2 hours untuk critical Monthly
Access Control Compliance >98% Quarterly
Policy Compliance Rate >95% Quarterly
Training Completion Rate 100% per tahun Quarterly
Vulnerability Remediation Time <30 hari untuk critical Monthly

6.2 Implement Continuous Monitoring

  • Real-time security monitoring via SIEM
  • Monthly vulnerability scans
  • Quarterly penetration tests
  • Regular access control reviews
  • Automated compliance checks

6.3 Internal Audit Program

Audit Planning:

  • Conduct internal audits minimum quarterly
  • Audit setiap area controls dalam 12 bulan
  • Use internal audit checklist based on ISO 27001:2022
  • Maintain audit records & evidence

Audit Process:

  1. Plan audit scope & schedule
  2. Collect & review evidence
  3. Interview personnel
  4. Document findings & non-conformances
  5. Report kepada management
  6. Track corrective actions

6.4 Management Review

Conduct formal management review minimum annually:

  • Review ISMS performance & KPIs
  • Review risk assessment & treatment status
  • Review audit findings & corrective actions
  • Evaluate stakeholder feedback
  • Assess external & internal changes (regulatory, business, technology)
  • Determine improvement opportunities
  • Approve budget untuk ISMS improvements

6.5 Continuous Improvement Loop

PDCA Model (Plan-Do-Check-Act):
  • Plan: Design ISMS & controls
  • Do: Implement controls
  • Check: Monitor & measure performance
  • Act: Improve based on findings

7. 14 Kontrol Utama ISO 27001:2022 & Implementasi

📌 Catatan: ISO 27001:2022 memiliki 93 kontrol. 14 kontrol berikut adalah yang paling critical dan sering diimplementasikan lebih dulu.

A.5: Organizational Controls (Contoh 4 Kontrol Penting)

A.5.1 - Information Security Policies

Requirement: Establish dan maintain documented information security policies

Implementation Checklist:
  • ☐ Develop master Information Security Policy
  • ☐ Get board/executive approval
  • ☐ Communicate to all staff
  • ☐ Include ISO 27001:2022 scope & objectives
  • ☐ Define roles & responsibilities
  • ☐ Review & update annually

A.5.2 - Information Security Roles & Responsibilities

Requirement: Define dan assign security roles

Key Roles:
  • CISO/Information Security Officer: Overall responsibility
  • Process Owners: Responsible untuk security in their function
  • IT Security Team: Implement & maintain technical controls
  • Data Protection Officer (DPO): If applicable for data protection
  • Internal Auditor: Verify compliance

A.5.3 - Segregation of Duties

Requirement: Ensure segregation of duties to prevent unauthorized actions

Implementation Examples:
  • ☐ Requestor ≠ Approver ≠ Implementer (access requests)
  • ☐ Developer ≠ System Administrator (code deployment)
  • ☐ Finance requestor ≠ payment approver ≠ processor
  • ☐ Review segregation of duties in mission-critical systems

A.6: People Controls (Contoh 2 Kontrol Penting)

A.6.1 - Screening & Onboarding

Requirement: Background checks & security training untuk new employees

Implementation:
  • ☐ Conduct background check sebelum hire (sesuai regulasi)
  • ☐ Mandatory security awareness training sebelum access diberi
  • ☐ Signed acceptable use policy
  • ☐ Document training completion

A.6.2 - Security Awareness & Training

Requirement: Regular security training untuk semua staff

Training Program:
  • ☐ Mandatory annual training untuk all staff
  • ☐ Role-specific training (developer, admin, finance, HR)
  • ☐ Phishing simulation & awareness campaigns
  • ☐ New hire onboarding training
  • ☐ Training completion tracking & records

A.7: Physical Controls (Contoh 2 Kontrol Penting)

A.7.1 - Physical Security Perimeter

Requirement: Protect facilities from unauthorized physical access

Implementation:
  • ☐ Data center: Fencing, gates, guards, CCTV
  • ☐ Server rooms: Key card access, badge access logs
  • ☐ Office security: Reception, visitor logs, ID badges
  • ☐ Regular physical security audits

A.7.2 - Physical Asset Management

Requirement: Inventory dan protect physical assets

Implementation:
  • ☐ Maintain asset inventory (servers, laptops, mobile devices)
  • ☐ Asset tagging & tracking system
  • ☐ Secure disposal of equipment (hard drive destruction)
  • ☐ Anti-theft measures

A.8: Technical Controls (Contoh 4 Kontrol Penting)

A.8.2 - Access Control

Requirement: Grant access berdasarkan need-to-know principle

Implementation:
  • ☐ Implement role-based access control (RBAC)
  • ☐ User provisioning & de-provisioning process
  • ☐ Regular access reviews (minimum quarterly)
  • ☐ Multi-factor authentication (MFA) untuk sensitive systems
  • ☐ Default deny principle untuk system access

A.8.3 - Cryptography

Requirement: Encrypt sensitive data

Implementation:
  • ☐ Encryption at rest: Database encryption, file encryption, disk encryption
  • ☐ Encryption in transit: TLS/SSL untuk all communications
  • ☐ Key management: Secure key generation, storage, rotation
  • ☐ Document encryption strategy & key management policy

A.8.10 - Information Security Event Logging

Requirement: Log dan monitor security-relevant events

Implementation:
  • ☐ Implement SIEM (Security Information & Event Management)
  • ☐ Log collection from all systems (servers, firewalls, etc.)
  • ☐ Centralized log storage & analysis
  • ☐ Real-time alerting untuk suspicious activities
  • ☐ Log retention minimum 12 bulan
  • ☐ Regular log review & investigation

A.8.16 - Incident Management

Requirement: Detect dan respond to security incidents

Implementation:
  • ☐ Develop incident response plan & procedures
  • ☐ Define incident classification & severity levels
  • ☐ Establish incident response team & hotline
  • ☐ Incident detection & notification process
  • ☐ Incident investigation & forensics procedures
  • ☐ Post-incident review & lessons learned
  • ☐ Maintain incident records for audit

8. Risk Management & Mitigation Strategy

8.1 Risk Assessment Process

Step 1: Identify Assets

  • Critical business systems & applications
  • Sensitive data repositories
  • Infrastructure components
  • Key personnel & roles

Step 2: Identify Threats

  • Cyber attacks (malware, ransomware, DDoS)
  • Insider threats (disgruntled employees)
  • Accidental data exposure (misconfiguration)
  • Natural disasters (flood, earthquake)
  • Third-party breaches

Step 3: Identify Vulnerabilities

  • Outdated software & systems
  • Weak access controls
  • Lack of encryption
  • Poor change management
  • Limited monitoring capabilities

Step 4: Evaluate Risk

Risk = Likelihood × Impact

Example:
Threat: Ransomware attack
Asset: Customer database
Likelihood: Medium (3/5)
Impact: Critical (5/5) - $1M loss + regulatory fine
Risk Score: 15/25 (HIGH RISK)

8.2 Risk Treatment Options

Treatment Type Action Example
Mitigate Implement control to reduce risk Implement MFA to reduce password breach risk
Accept Accept risk with documented approval Accept risk of legacy system compromise (planned for retirement)
Avoid Eliminate activity causing risk Stop using insecure payment processing method
Transfer Transfer risk via insurance or outsourcing Buy cyber insurance, outsource to managed security provider

8.3 Risk Register Template

Risk ID: R-001 Description: Ransomware attack on customer database Asset Affected: Customer DB, Email System Threat: Cyber attack Vulnerability: Outdated patching process Likelihood: Medium (3/5) Impact: Critical (5/5) Current Controls: Basic antivirus, Firewall Risk Score: 15/25 (HIGH) Treatment: Mitigate Planned Controls: - Implement email filtering & anti-ransomware - Automated patching process - Daily incremental backup - Incident response plan Target Completion: End Q2 2026 Owner: IT Security Director Status: In Progress

8.4 Residual Risk Monitoring

After implementing controls, monitor residual risk:

  • Track residual risk score vs. risk appetite
  • Review risk treatment effectiveness quarterly
  • Adjust controls jika residual risk terlalu tinggi
  • Report to management review

10. Persiapan Audit Internal & Sertifikasi

10.1 Internal Audit Program

10.1.1 Audit Planning

  • Define audit scope based on risk profile
  • Create 12-month audit plan (cover all areas)
  • Assign qualified internal auditors
  • Develop audit checklists based on ISO 27001:2022
  • Schedule audits quarterly minimum

10.1.2 Audit Execution

  • Pre-audit: Notify auditee, gather documentation
  • Audit: Review documentation, interview staff, observe processes
  • Findings: Document non-conformances & opportunities for improvement
  • Report: Prepare audit report dengan findings & recommendations
  • Follow-up: Track corrective action completion

10.1.3 Audit Checklist Example

AUDIT CHECKLIST: Access Control (A.8.2) 1. Access Control Policy ☐ Policy exists & is documented ☐ Policy covers user provisioning & de-provisioning ☐ Policy defines authorization levels ☐ Policy is approved & communicated 2. User Access Management ☐ Access request process is documented ☐ All access requests are approved before provisioning ☐ Access logs are maintained ☐ Quarterly access reviews are conducted ☐ De-provisioning is done within 5 days of termination 3. Multi-Factor Authentication ☐ MFA is implemented for sensitive systems ☐ MFA is enforced for remote access ☐ MFA is documented in policy 4. Evidence Required: ☐ Access policy document ☐ Sample access request forms (5 recent) ☐ Access review evidence (last quarter) ☐ De-provisioning checklist samples ☐ MFA configuration evidence

10.2 Certification Audit Preparation

10.2.1 Choose Certification Body

Select accredited certification body (examples):

  • Bureau Veritas (BV)
  • TÜV Rheinland
  • SGS
  • DNV GL
  • Local auditors (di Indonesia)

10.2.2 Pre-audit Readiness Assessment

Conduct mock audit sebelum certification audit:

  • Internal audit review semua kontrol
  • Identify & close gaps
  • Ensure documentation is complete
  • Prepare evidence untuk audit
  • Conduct management review

10.2.3 Certification Audit Stages

Stage 1
Documentation review (1-2 hari) - Auditor review policies, procedures, documentation
Stage 2
Main audit (3-5 hari) - On-site audit of all controls, interviews, sampling
Review
Findings & corrective actions - Address minor findings within 4-6 weeks
Certificate
Issuance - Certificate valid for 3 years with annual surveillance audits

10.3 Audit Readiness Checklist

Information Security Policy is documented & approved
Risk assessment is complete & documented
Kontrol telah diimplementasikan per risk assessment
All required procedures are documented
Evidence exists untuk semua kontrol (training records, audit logs, etc.)
Internal audits have been conducted
Management review telah dilakukan
Corrective actions untuk gaps telah dikerjakan
ISMS is operational minimal 2 bulan sebelum audit
All roles & responsibilities are defined & communicated

10.4 Post-Certification Maintenance

  • Conduct annual surveillance audits (certification body requirement)
  • Maintain internal audit program (quarterly minimum)
  • Keep documentation up-to-date
  • Manage corrective actions for audit findings
  • Continue management review & improvement
  • Renew certification after 3 years

Kesimpulan & Next Steps

Implementasi ISO 27001:2022 adalah journey, bukan destination. Untuk sukses:

  • Secure Executive Support: Dapatkan dukungan C-level sebagai first step
  • Take Risk-Based Approach: Focus pada highest-risk areas dulu
  • Document Everything: Maintain comprehensive records untuk audit
  • Engage All Stakeholders: Security adalah tanggung jawab semua orang
  • Continuous Improvement: ISMS is never "done" - always improving
  • Allocate Resources: Budget & people yang cukup adalah critical
🎯 Recommended Implementation Timeline:
  • Phase 1 (Persiapan): 0-4 minggu
  • Phase 2 (Perencanaan): 4-8 minggu
  • Phase 3 (Implementasi): 8-24 minggu
  • Phase 4 (Optimization): Ongoing
  • Total: 6-12 bulan untuk certification-ready
