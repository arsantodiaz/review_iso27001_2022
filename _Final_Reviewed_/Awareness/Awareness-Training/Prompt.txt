Prompt: A 3D claymation style render of an open, minimalist notebook floating gently in the center. Small, colorful glowing idea nodes and papers made of matte clay are popping out of the pages. Soft studio lighting, highly detailed clay texture with subtle fingerprints, vibrant but pastel colors.
